Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Reyes Painting & Pressure Cleaning ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit through our estimate request form or WhatsApp: name, phone number, email address, and project details you choose to share.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited, collected automatically for site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to estimate requests and schedule work.
  • To communicate with you about your project via phone, email, or WhatsApp.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form and WhatsApp link route through third-party services (form processing and WhatsApp/Meta) that have their own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies as well.

Data Retention

We retain estimate and contact information only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or complete a project, and for basic bookkeeping records afterward.
